Welcome to City of Crime

City of Crime is an online role-playing game and community where the players strive for fame in a virtual mob industry.

You start the game as a complete nobody with nothing but your hopes and dreams. You can raid gangs, sell a few gigs, and begin to build a mafia. Play your cards right and you'll soon be conquering the world, cutting chart-topping records, and leaving a wake of tantalizing scandals behind you!
